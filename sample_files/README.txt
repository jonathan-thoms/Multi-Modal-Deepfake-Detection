Sample Files for Deepfake Detection Demonstration

This directory contains sample files for testing the Image and Audio deepfake detection models.

CONTENTS:

1. Images (from 'combined-real-and-fake-faces')
   - images/real/: 5 real images
   - images/fake/: 5 fake images

2. Audio (from 'AudioDeepfakeDetection/TestEval')
   - audio/real/: 3 real (bonafide) audio files
   - audio/fake/: 5 fake (spoof) audio files
   Note: Only 3 real audio samples were available in the repository.

3. Video
   - video/real/: 5 real samples (Downloaded from Google's sample media)
   - video/fake/: 5 fake samples (Placeholders copied from real videos due to lack of direct download links)
